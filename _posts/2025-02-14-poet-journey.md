**Welcome to my story.** I share it not to disturb your peace but to guide you toward passion—your compass when life leads you astray.

I’ll take you to the **Hidden Garden of Eden**—a place I once called home but lost to the temptation of sin. This is the journey of a poet, **banished from paradise**, lost in the wilderness of work, and redeemed by the 3 Ps: **passion, patience, and persistence**.

---

## Discovering [My](/about/) Literary Paradise: Writing as a  Young Poet

![Azerbaijani poet discovering his love for writing in a garden paradise](/assets/images/posts/poetjourney/03-eden.png)

Once, I lived in a **literary paradise**. I discovered my love for **writing** in high school—a love so deep, it felt like magic. My beloved, the Word, and I were like two dreamers, rowing a paper boat with a pencil as our oar, across the boundless ocean of imagination. Together, we found the **Garden of Eden**. 

During my student years, I dwelled in a sacred paradise of words. In that haven, I was Adam, and the Word was my Eve. Together, we wove beauty, crafting poems and stories that breathed life into my dreams. But one day, temptation whispered:

'Let us taste the forbidden fruit.'

Drawn to the Tree of Work, I bit into its fruit—the relentless pursuit of money. In that single act, I severed my bond with the Word. My paradise crumbled, leaving me exiled in a harsh world far from my literary dreams.

---

## Falling into the Corporate Trap: Losing My Writing Passion

![Poet trapped in an office cubicle, staring out the window, longing to escape](/assets/images/posts/poetjourney/02-banishment.png)

Leaving university felt like leaving Eden. My passion for writing in Azerbaijani Turkic—a language marginalized in Iran’s education system—offered no path to a stable livelihood. Few people could read or appreciate [Azerbaijani literature](https://ishiq.net/author/pirikliartan), leaving my words to echo unheard and my dreams out of reach.

Out of necessity, I strayed from the right path. I chased money, mistaking it for the ultimate success, unable to see it as merely a reward for pursuing our passion. In doing so, I abandoned the Word and stepped into the unrelenting grind of corporate life.

That was my original sin—the moment I lost my way. I became an exile, trapped in a world of endless routines, drained of joy and creativity

---

## The Search for Purpose: Failing to Replace My Creative Fire

![The search for purpose](/assets/images/posts/poetjourney/04-escape.png)

As the weight of routine grew unbearable, I promised myself I’d break free within two years. But two years turned into ten. I became a “hell boy,” trapped in a soul-crushing office that suffocated my spirit and stifled my creativity.

Desperate to escape the monotony, I strayed further from my true passion, wandering through the endless search for something new:

- I tried language teaching, but it failed to ignite a spark.
- I explored Python programming and [data science](https://datacamp.com), only to quit.
- I ventured into [web development](https://www.pirikli.com/pirikli/), hoping it might lead me to fulfilment.

Each path taught me lessons but left me hollow, unable to replace the fire of my first love. Without faith in my true calling—and consumed by the struggle to make a living—I kept straying further, repeating my sin. I chased substitutes for my passion, only to feel more lost and disconnected from the path I was meant to walk.

---

## Rediscovering My True Calling: Writing as My Path to Freedom

![A lone traveler walking a winding path in the wilderness, seeking direction](/assets/images/posts/poetjourney/05-repentance.png)

Each detour whispered the same truth:  
"Every path is strewn with struggles and obstacles. There’s no shortcut to success, no escape from the need for time and patience. But only on your true path will passion sustain you."

One day, an inner voice awakened me:  
"Without repentance, you’ll remain lost. Challenges will come, but if you return to your true calling, passion will be your companion, helping you persist through every trial."

My diversions weren’t failures—they were lessons. They taught me that persistence alone isn’t enough; it must be rooted in passion. And passion was waiting, all along, on the path I had strayed from. With humility, I repented and took the first step back toward my rightful path, ready to embrace its struggles.

---

## Returning to Writing: Finding Redemption in Creativity

![A lush garden with an open notebook and pen, symbolizing creative redemption](/assets/images/posts/poetjourney/06-redemption.png)

Amid the **corporate grind**, I finally found my way back to the **Hidden Garden of Eden**. Slowly and patiently, I returned to my first love: **writing**.  
I began to heal myself with the **3 Ps**: **passion**, **patience**, and **persistence**. Each step felt like therapy, reminding me that the journey was the paradise I’d been searching for all along.  
As **Adam reunited with Eve**, I reentered my Eden. This time, I refused the forbidden fruit of chasing money for its own sake. Instead, I wrote for the joy of creation—for the beauty of weaving words into worlds.

Now, I walk this path with passion, patience, and persistence, finding happiness in every step.

---

## A Message to Creatives: Follow Your Passion with Patience

![AI-Enhanced Creative Writer, Storyteller and Poet is guiding other writers who are seeking their passion](/assets/images/posts/poetjourney/07-call.png)

To all who seek their true path: step boldly forward. The road will teach you to walk, stumble, and rise again. Embrace each challenge—they are the rhythms of your sacred journey.

Welcome, fellow pilgrim. Like you, I’ve wandered far and returned to my first love—**words**. I’m walking alongside dear companions like **language learning**, exploring the storyland where creativity blooms.

This website is my sanctuary—a garden where I nurture my dreams as an **Azerbaijani poet**, **writer**, and **storyteller**. Here, I invite you to join me in:

- **Journaling My Journey**: Sharing reflections, struggles, and victories.
- **LearNotes**: Insights from **language learning** and **storytelling**.
- **Creating Stories and Poems**: Tales that inspire, educate, and spark imagination.

Follow my journey. Together, we’ll explore a world where passion lights our way, creativity shapes our future, and every step forward is a reward. Let’s build a **storyland** where **words** inspire, teach, and unite us all.
